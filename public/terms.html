9. Case Law Data — Accuracy & Limitations

LexAI's case law search is powered by CourtListener (Free Law Project) and Harvard Caselaw Access Project. These are reputable public legal databases, but:

  • Coverage is not identical to commercial databases such as Westlaw or LexisNexis
  • Harvard CAP covers cases up to approximately 2020; more recent cases may not appear
  • CourtListener coverage depends on court publishing practices and may have gaps
  • Case summaries and excerpts are automated and may not capture full holdings
  • Citations should be independently verified before inclusion in legal filings
  • LexAI does not verify that cited cases are good law (i.e. not overruled)

LexAI is not responsible for decisions made in reliance on case law search results. Always verify case citations through official court records or authoritative legal databases before professional use.
